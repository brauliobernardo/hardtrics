Our Company

Hardtrics is an independent company located in Paarden Eiland

Our clients come first

History

Established in 2005 as WS Gear & Engineering cc, we now have merged with our sister company Zakula Electrical Wholesalers cc, established 2006 to form a stronger unit known as HARDTRICS HARDWARE & ELECTRICAL SUPPLIES (PTY)LTD in 2018

With our new name and new structure, we have grown our product range even bigger to better suit our customers needs, creating a one stop shop.

Staff

Our staff are dedicated to providing high quality service and building good relationships with our customers, creating great partnerships.

We are committed to providing a high quality service to all our customers. Striving to become a leading company in the electrical and engineering industries

Customers

Building a win- win relationship with our customers, providing them with friendly honest and efficient service and competitive pricing.

Our customer base includes property developers, electrical/building contractors, retail outlets, municipalities, general public and many more various industries in and around Cape Town

Mission

Our mission is to provide customers with reliable, effective and efficient service. Expand our customer bases and grow the company from strength to strength as a team of excellence.
