History

During 2005 in true entrepreneurial spirit Sydney Chamberlain started “WS Gear & Engineering CC” situated in Paarden Eiland. A year later he launched his second venture – “Zakula Electrical CC” situated in the same premises as WS Gear & Engineering cc.

Candice Matthee joined Sydney as part of the management team and together they grew the two companies side by side, building the reputation of both companies on good service, supported by quality products at competitive prices.

Fast forward to 2018 and Sydney and Candice have made a major decision to merge WS Gear & Engineering and Zakula Electrical into a new entity called “HARDTRICS HARDWARE & ELECTRICAL SUPPLIES (PTY) LTD.”

Clients now experience the same great service and quality products at competitive prices, but with the additional value of a bigger and more diversified product range with less administrative burden and a much improved customer relationship experience.

“We are totally focused on providing our clients with a one stop shop experience that exceeds expectations”.

Sydney Chamberlain
